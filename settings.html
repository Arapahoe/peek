Peek settings

Document viewer

You can change the viewer used to Word Documents, Excel Spreadsheets, and PowerPoint presentations. The default is Google Docs.

Note: This does not affect PDFs, RTFs, and links to Google Docs files.

Advanced settings

Getting Started

Peek shows previews over file download links. Just hover your mouse over a link to a file, and the preview will appear.

Try clicking here and hover over some results.

You can click the full-screen button on the popup to open the preview in a new window.

To open Peek settings, click the settings gear on any popup, or click the Peek icon in the toolbar.
