Getting Started

Peek shows previews over file download links. Just hover your mouse over a link to a file, and the preview will appear.

Try clicking here and hover over some results.

You can click the full-screen button on the popup to open the preview in a new window.

To open Peek settings, click the settings gear on any popup, or click the Peek icon in the toolbar. You can also click the button below.

Open Peek settings »

Supported files

Peek can create previews for the following file types:

  • PDF and RTF files
  • Word documents (.doc, .docx)
  • Excel spreadsheets (.xls, .xlsx)
  • PowerPoint files (.ppt, .pptx)
  • WebM, MP4, GIFV, and OGG/OGV video
  • MP3 and WAV Audio
