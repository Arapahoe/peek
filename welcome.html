What's New?

Peek now supports Gfycat links and Imgur GIFV links, and previews both as video.

Peek now shows previews for .m4v, .m4a, and .oga files.

Fixes for Google Docs links not working.

Getting Started

Peek shows previews over file download links. Just hover your mouse over a link to a file, and the preview will appear.

Try clicking here and hover over some results.

Peek supports PDF files, Word documents, Excel spreadsheets, PowerPoint presentations, HTML5 video (WebM, MP4, Ogg), Flash video files, and HTML5 audio (MP3, WAV).
